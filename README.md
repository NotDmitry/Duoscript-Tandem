## Duoscript-Tandem

### About this project

**Duoscript-Tandem** is your best friend during the tough times of preparing for technical interviews.
Its primary aim is to provide you with a variety of exercises that will aid in the development
and enhancement of your hard-skills. The app contains multiple widgets and a dashboard for tracking your activity.

## What we’re proud of

- Successfully adopting a new technology stack and growing our expertise along the way
- Embracing continuous learning and exploring new tools and approaches
- Strong teamwork and open communication
- Supporting each other and sharing knowledge across the team
- Delivering a solid product through collaboration and mutual trust
