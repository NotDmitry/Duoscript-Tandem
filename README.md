## Duoscript-Tandem

### About this project

**Duoscript-Tandem** is your best friend during the tough times of preparing for technical interviews.
Its primary aim is to provide you with a variety of exercises that will aid in the development
and enhancement of your hard-skills. The app contains multiple widgets and a dashboard for tracking your activity.
